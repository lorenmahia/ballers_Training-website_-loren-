    • Ballers Baskteball was started in 2019 by Loren Mahia to train people how to play basketball better. This organisation was founded on two key principles, Inclusion and Diligence.
    Inclusion means that we believe anyone can play basketball, despite the age, race, gender or religion.
    Diligence means that we encourage all our clients to follow our program to the best of thier ability and work hard for what they want.
    Our energetic and able staff includes former NBA coaches, former All-start athletes who hail from all over the globe.
    We are located in Boston, Massachusetts with franchises in Chicago, New York, San Andreas and Tokyo.
